About

After trying (and failing) to manage 5+ Twitter X roleplay accounts at once, and after getting locked out of my primary RP account because Twitter is going to hell, I decided to start from scratch with a new account.

And then I got so sick of Twitter X that it was enough to make me leave entirely, and so I decided to really start from scratch.

Born Of Velocity encapsulates all characters I've ever roleplayed as, and all characters I plan to roleplay in the future. It's essentially an indefinitely large multimuse profile.

This website will serve as an incomprehensive list of characters as well as a collection of short crossover headcanon-y stories written by myself.
