About

After trying (and failing) to manage 5+ Twitter roleplay accounts at once, and after getting locked out of my primary RP account because Twitter is going to hell, I decided to start from scratch.

Born Of Velocity encapsulates all characters I've ever roleplayed as, and all characters I plan to roleplay in the future. It's essentially an indefinitely large multimuse account.
